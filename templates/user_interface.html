Welcome to our Sports Matching Website

Find your perfect sports match!

To get started, simply use the menu button located in the top right corner. From there, you can:

  • Create a new group for a specific sport, location, and time
  • Join existing groups based on your preferences
  • Check and manage your profile

Our website works by allowing you to create or join groups based on your location and sports preferences. When creating a group, you can choose the sport, location, start time, and end time. Once the end time is reached, a voting system will be activated, allowing users to rate each other based on their skills and fair play.
